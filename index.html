About Me

My Story

I have a Bachelor of Education in Physical and Health Education and a Master of Arts in Educational Leadership from McGill University. With six years of experience as a secondary school educator, I’ve gained a deep understanding of curriculum development and teaching principles. My passion for innovation led me to obtain design thinking certificates from IBM and IDEO.

Driven by a passion for innovation and guided by a deep commitment to educational excellence, I returned to McGill to pursue a Master of Arts in Educational Leadership, with a focus on change management, mentorship, and community-based learning.

Currently, I am proud to be part of the Digital Research Alliance of Canada, where I contribute to maintaining and expanding national Digital Research Infrastructure training operations and strategy. My focus includes Advanced Research Computing, Research Data Management, Research Software, and Cybersecurity.
